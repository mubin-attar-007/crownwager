# Responsible Gambling & Scope

## What OddsAway is

OddsAway is an **informational analytics tool**. It surfaces model predictions, expected-value
estimates, odds comparisons, and arbitrage calculations for educational and entertainment purposes.

## What OddsAway is NOT

- It is **not** a sportsbook. It does not accept, place, or settle wagers.
- It does **not** hold, transfer, or process money. There is no wallet, deposit, or payout.
- It does **not** provide financial advice or guarantee any outcome. Models can be wrong; odds move.

Because no funds change hands and no bets are executed, gambling-operator obligations (licensing,
KYC/AML, payment processing) are **out of scope** by design. Any future move toward real-money
functionality would require a full legal/compliance review before a single line of code.

## Player protections in the product

- **18+** and responsible-gambling notices appear on every betting-related surface and in the footer.
- Stake recommendations default to **fractional (half) Kelly** to temper variance, and are always
  framed as informational.
- Every predictive API response includes a disclaimer string.

## Get help

If gambling is causing you or someone you know harm, call **1-800-GAMBLER** (US) or contact your local
problem-gambling helpline. Bet only what you can afford to lose.
